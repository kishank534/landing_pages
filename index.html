Maintaining fitness while working from home

Date:20 may 2020
Time:4:00 p.m.-5:00 p.m.

About the Workshop

  1. Create your routine to maintain mental and physical fitness while working from home
  2. Set expectations in both professional and personal areas
  3. Take specific steps to prevent burnout 4. Plan your nutrition requirement during the day

What You Will Learn

Create Better health condition

Improve your relationship with food, increase your energy, balance hormones, or feel better overall

Creating a healthy Lifestyle

Transform your body, health and life for years, rather than days by creating a healthy lifestyle

Discover wellness vision

Create an actionable path to bring wellness vision in your life and start living a healthy live.

About the Speaker

Tracy McGuire

Fitness Nutrition Expert

I hold a professional certification in Fitness Nutrition and Group Exercise from the International Sports Science Association. In addition, I have a B.A. from the University of Virginia and a M.Ed. from George Mason University.
